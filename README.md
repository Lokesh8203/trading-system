## Key Trading Principles (Summary)

### The 10 Commandments of Trading

1. **Trend is Your Friend**: Trade with trend until clear reversal
2. **Volume Confirms Price**: High volume validates moves
3. **Multiple Confirmations**: Wait for confluence before entry
4. **Risk Management First**: Protect capital above all else
5. **Patience and Discipline**: Wait for high-probability setups
6. **Timeframe Matters**: Higher timeframes more reliable
7. **No Holy Grail**: No single indicator/pattern works always
8. **Adapt and Learn**: Continuously improve and refine
9. **Capital Preservation**: Avoid big losses, compound small wins
10. **Context is Key**: Consider broader market environment

### Essential Risk Parameters

- **Risk per trade**: 1% (conservative) to 2% (aggressive)
- **Portfolio heat**: Maximum 6% across all open positions
- **Risk-Reward ratio**: Minimum 1:2, prefer 1:3
- **Stop loss method**: 1.2 × ATR(5, RMA)
- **Target**: 2× stop loss distance minimum
- **Partial profits**: Book 50% at target, trail 50%
- **Daily stop**: -2% of account
- **Weekly stop**: -5% of account
- **Monthly stop**: -10% of account

### Standard Indicator Settings

- **Moving Averages**: 9, 21, 34, 50, 100, 200
- **MACD**: 12, 26, 9
- **RSI**: 14 periods
- **Bollinger Bands**: 20 periods, 2 SD
- **Supertrend**: 10, hl2, 2.1
- **ATR**: 5 periods, RMA

## Practical Implementation Workflow

### Phase 1: Setup (Before Trading)
1. Review **TRADING_KNOWLEDGE_BASE.md** thoroughly
2. Choose 3-5 patterns/setups to specialize in
3. Master 3-4 complementary indicators
4. Create watchlist of stocks/instruments
5. Set up charts with chosen indicators
6. Print **QUICK_REFERENCE_GUIDE.md** or keep digital copy accessible

### Phase 2: Practice (Paper Trading)
1. Apply learned patterns in paper trading
2. Use **QUICK_REFERENCE_GUIDE.md** for decisions
3. Document every trade in journal
4. Review trades weekly
5. Calculate win rate and average R:R
6. Refine approach based on results
7. Continue for minimum 100 paper trades or 3 months

### Phase 3: Live Trading (Small Size)
1. Start with 20-30% of planned position size
2. Use risk management formulas from **ADVANCED_STRATEGIES.md**
3. Follow checklists from **QUICK_REFERENCE_GUIDE.md**
4. Maintain strict discipline
5. Review performance monthly
6. Gradually increase size as consistency improves
7. Never skip risk management steps

### Phase 4: Advanced Implementation
1. Incorporate advanced strategies selectively
2. Implement pyramiding in trending markets
3. Use RS analysis for stock selection
4. Apply multi-timeframe analysis rigorously
5. Utilize professional risk management
6. Continue learning and adapting
