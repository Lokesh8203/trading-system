## RISK WARNINGS

### ⚠️ HIGHEST RISKS:

**1. Structural Shift Risk (BIGGEST DANGER)**
- Historical means may be OBSOLETE
- Silver, Copper fundamentally changed roles
- Trading toward old mean = trading toward wrong target
- **Mitigation:** Size 50% smaller than historical data suggests

**2. Margin Call Risk**
- Overnight gaps can trigger margin calls
- MCX has NO circuit breakers
- Weekend gaps can be 5-10%
- **Mitigation:** Keep 40% cash reserve, never use full margin

**3. Correlation Breakdown Risk**
- During extreme volatility, both legs can move against you
- Pair trade becomes directional loss
- **Mitigation:** Stop loss on EACH leg, not just on ratio

**4. Liquidity Risk**
- Mini contracts may widen spreads during volatility
- Zinc has lower liquidity
- Exits may be worse than entry
- **Mitigation:** Trade during high-liquidity hours, use limit orders

**5. Rollover Cost Risk**
- MCX futures expire monthly
- Rollover = 1-2% cost per month
- Holding 6 months = 6-12% drag on returns
- **Mitigation:** Factor rollover cost into targets, don't over-hold
