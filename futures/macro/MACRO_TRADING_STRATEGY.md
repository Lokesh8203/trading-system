## ✅ The Framework Built

I've created a system that answers **5 critical questions** before any macro trade:

### 1. **Is the thesis historically valid?**
- Support/resistance analysis (where did price bounce before?)
- Drawdown recovery rates (after X% drop, how often did it recover?)
- Volatility regime (is market calm or explosive?)

### 2. **What's the expected value?**
- Scenario analysis (bull/base/bear outcomes)
- Probability-weighted returns
- Risk/reward ratio

### 3. **How much to risk?**
- Position sizing (NOT all-in, ever)
- Leverage calculation (2-5x max)
- Max risk per trade (10-20% of capital)

### 4. **How to enter?**
- Pyramid entry (scale in 1/3 at a time)
- Entry zones (not just one price)
- Confirmation levels

### 5. **When to exit?**
- Multiple targets (take profits in stages)
- Stop loss (even on conviction trades)
- Time-based exit (if thesis doesn't play out in X months)

## 🚨 Critical Lessons from Analysis

### Lesson 1: **NEVER Go All-In**

You said: "I can literally go all in and get 5x returns"

**Reality:** Yes, but one wrong move = -100%

**Smart approach:**
- Max 40% allocation per trade
- Max 4-5x leverage
- This still gives you 1.5-2x capital exposure
- But leaves buffer for:
  - Adding on dips
  - Other opportunities
  - Margin calls if wrong

**Example:**
- All-in: ₹12L × 5x leverage = ₹60L exposure → +10% = ₹6L profit BUT -10% = -₹6L (wiped out)
- Smart: ₹4.8L × 4x leverage = ₹19.2L exposure → +10% = ₹1.92L profit, -10% = -₹1.92L (still have ₹10L left)

### Lesson 2: **Conviction ≠ Certainty**

Your crude oil thesis might be right, but:
- **Expected value is negative** (-6.5%)
- This means even if thesis correct, position sizing/levels make it unprofitable
- Historical data says: wait for better entry

### Lesson 3: **Weekend Gap Risk is Real**

You mentioned: "Markets closed on weekends, overnight moves wipe accounts"

**Solution:**
- **Always use stop loss** (even on "sure" trades)
- Size position so stop loss = 10-20% max loss
- For Bank Nifty at 17.7% risk → TOO HIGH, reduce to 1 lot (8.8% risk)
- Keep 50% cash reserve for gap scenarios

### Lesson 4: **Historical Data > Gut Feel**

Bank Nifty "bottoming at 50k":
- **Data shows:** 50k NOT tested in recent 3 years
- Recent supports: ₹55K, ₹54.9K, ₹54.2K
- **Implication:** 50k is ASSUMPTION, not proven support
- **Action:** Enter in scale (1/3 now, 1/3 at ₹54.8K near proven support)
